Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Tanzania?

+

The best time to visit Tanzania depends on what you want to see. For wildlife viewing, the dry seasons (June to October and January to February) are ideal. The wildebeest migration occurs from July to October in the Serengeti.

How long should I plan for a safari?

+

We recommend a minimum of 5-7 days for a satisfying safari experience. This allows you to visit multiple parks and increases your chances of seeing diverse wildlife.

What vaccinations do I need?

+

Required vaccinations include Yellow Fever. Recommended vaccinations include Hepatitis A & B, Typhoid, and Malaria prophylaxis. Please consult your healthcare provider for personalized advice.

What should I pack for safari?

+

Essential items include neutral-colored clothing, sun protection, insect repellent, comfortable walking shoes, and a good camera. We provide a detailed packing list upon booking.

Are the safaris suitable for children?

+

Yes, many of our safaris are family-friendly. We recommend children be at least 6 years old and can customize itineraries to suit family needs. Some luxury lodges have specific family programs and facilities.
